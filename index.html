A Little About Me

Hey there! I'm Cassie Meffert, a Madison area photographer with a passion to share my photography art.

I first got started with photography when I was pregnant with my first child. Believe it or not, I bought the camera for my husband! It wasn't long before I was taking every opportunity to document my new babe's life. However, the real inspiration came six short months later when my sister passed away from breast cancer. It allowed me to reflect on the every day beauty of special moments and memories of the time we had together. It sparked a fire in me to capture those personal moments I reflected on. I also became sensitized to so many picturesque scenes around me that had previously been the mundane. I am so excited to be able to share my vision with you!
